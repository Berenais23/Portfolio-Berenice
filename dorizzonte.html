Prof.Menichelli e Armenti

Our project led to the creation of an original alphabet, inspired by photographs of the aerial tram line that runs from Roma Termini to Centocelle. We captured images of the unique cables, structures and configurations visible along this route. Each letter of the alphabet was designed to reflect the shapes and geometries characteristic of the tram's power lines. The result is a typography that combines art and urbanism, transforming functional elements of the city into an innovative of writing and innovative signs.
